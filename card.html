Eagle

Eagles have strong vision. They have the ability to focus on something up to five kilometers away.

  • Scientific Name: Haliaeetus pelagicus
  • Weight: 5 to 9 kg
  • Height: 2 ft 9 in to 3 ft 5 in
  • Conservation status: Vulnerable

Steller's sea eagle (Haliaeetus pelagicus) is a large bird of prey in the family Accipitridae that lives in coastal northeastern Asia and mainly preys on fish and water birds. On average, it is the heaviest eagle in the world.
